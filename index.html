Week 8: May 22 - 26

This was a roller coaster week for me. On Monday, we did a code along with Music History. We added Firebase to the project and made a complete CRUD application. CRUD means Create, Replace, Update and Delete. This is what we need for our mid-term capstones so this was a very important code along.


Then, beginning on Tuesday, we were placed into groups and began the Movie assignment. We had to incorporate everything that we were shown in the Music History code along. As usual, I felt like the underdog in our group. You'd think I'd be used to it by now, but I'm not. It boggles my mind about how much my classmates know compared to me.


We worked as a group until Friday afternoon when we presented our projects. I think one team out of five had everything completed so I didn't feel too bad.

Twice during this Movie group project, the assignment changed. The first time, they added a couple of different things. And then, the second time, they changed all of our code. Well, not changed, we just had to give our code to another team and in turn, we got another teams code. It simulated what will probably happen in the "real world".
